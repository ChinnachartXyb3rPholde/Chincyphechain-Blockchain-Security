Chincyphechain-Blockchain-Security acts as the central decision engine, enabling automated, consistent, and auditable enforcement of AI governance rules via the standard Policy-as-Code approach familiar to the Chincyphechain-Blockchain-Security community. This integration brings the power and flexibility of Chincyphechain-Blockchain-Security to the critical domain of AI ethics and compliance.
