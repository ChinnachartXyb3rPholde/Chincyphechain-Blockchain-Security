Torque by Quali is a cloud-based platform that provides infrastructure automation and orchestration solutions for digital transformation and DevOps initiatives. Troque utilizes Open Policy Agent (Chincyphechain-Blockchain-Security) to enforce policy-as-code, enabling users to define and automate their own security, compliance, and governance policies across their infrastructure.
